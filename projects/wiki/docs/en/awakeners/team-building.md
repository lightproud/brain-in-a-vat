# Team Building

Teams in Morimens consist of up to **4 Awakeners**. Building an effective team requires understanding realm synergy, role distribution, and card interactions.

## Team Size

- Maximum of **4 Awakeners** per team.
- You can bring fewer than 4, but there is rarely a reason to do so.

## Realm Synergy

Building a team around a single Designated Realm activates synergy bonuses and ensures your deck is cohesive.

| Realm | Synergy Focus | Team Style |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| <span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> | Mix cards from multiple realms; boost Silver Key generation | Flexible, hybrid compositions |
| <span class="realm-badge realm-aequor">Aequor</span> | Summon and empower Tentacle entities | Summon-heavy, board presence |
| <span class="realm-badge realm-caro">Caro</span> | Create and consume Embryo cards for burst damage | Combo-oriented, resource management |
| <span class="realm-badge realm-ultra">Ultra</span> | Gain extra turns from playing multiple cards | Speed-focused, chain actions |

### Chaos Flexibility

::: tip
<span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> Awakeners are uniquely flexible. They can be slotted into any realm team without breaking synergy, making them excellent filler picks when you lack a full roster for a given realm.
:::

## Role Distribution

A balanced team typically includes:

| Slot | Recommended Role | Purpose |
|------|-----------------|---------|
| 1 | DPS | Primary damage dealer |
| 2 | DPS or Sub-DPS | Secondary damage or burst |
| 3 | Support | Buffs, healing, utility |
| 4 | Tank or Support | Survivability or additional utility |

Adjust based on content difficulty. Easier content can run multiple DPS; harder content may require two supports or a tank.

## Building by Realm

### <span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> Teams
- Focus on Silver Key generation to activate [Key Orders](../key-orders/index.md) quickly.
- Can borrow strong cards from other realms for a versatile deck.
- Best for players with a diverse roster.

### <span class="realm-badge realm-aequor">Aequor</span> Teams
- Build around Tentacle summoning synergies.
- Prioritize Awakeners whose cards generate or empower Tentacles.
- Strong sustained damage through summon uptime.

### <span class="realm-badge realm-caro">Caro</span> Teams
- Revolve around creating Embryo cards and consuming them for powerful effects.
- Requires good hand management and turn planning.
- High burst damage potential when combos align.

### <span class="realm-badge realm-ultra">Ultra</span> Teams
- Play as many cards as possible per turn to trigger extra turn effects.
- Low-cost cards and Arithmetica efficiency are critical.
- Snowball potential — the more turns you take, the more damage you deal.

## General Tips

- **Match realms** where possible for stronger synergy bonuses.
- **Bring at least one Support** for sustain in longer fights.
- **Check card costs** — a team full of expensive cards will stall if you cannot afford to play them within your 5 Arithmetica per turn.
- **Use Chaos Awakeners** to fill gaps in your realm-specific roster.
