## Skill Unlock at Level 25

::: warning
Awakeners unlock an additional skill enhancement at **Level 25**. Make sure to level your core team members to at least 25 to access their full kit.
:::

Reaching Level 25 unlocks a passive or enhanced effect for one of the Awakener's Command Cards. The specific unlock varies by character.

## Leveling Priority

1. **Main DPS** — Level as high as possible for maximum damage output
2. **Sub-DPS / Secondary Carry** — Level to at least 25 for skill unlock
3. **Support** — Level to 25 for skill unlock; further levels add survivability
4. **Tank** — Benefits greatly from Constitution scaling; level after supports if resources are limited
