# Awakeners

**Awakeners** (唤醒体) are the playable characters in Morimens. Each Awakener belongs to a Designated Realm and fulfills a combat role on your team.

## Rarity

| Rarity | Pull Rate | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| <span class="rarity-ssr">SSR</span> | 3% | Highest rarity; 30-pull pity guaranteed |
| <span class="rarity-sr">SR</span> | — | Mid-tier rarity |
| <span class="rarity-r">R</span> | — | Common rarity |

::: tip
The SSR rate was increased from 1% to 3% during development, making Morimens one of the more generous gacha games on the market.
:::

## Designated Realms

Every Awakener is aligned with one of four Designated Realms:

| Realm | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| <span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> | Flexible realm that mixes cards from multiple realms and boosts Silver Key generation |
| <span class="realm-badge realm-aequor">Aequor</span> | Deep-sea realm focused on summoning Tentacle entities |
| <span class="realm-badge realm-caro">Caro</span> | Flesh realm that creates and consumes Embryo cards |
| <span class="realm-badge realm-ultra">Ultra</span> | Dimensional realm that gains extra turns from playing cards |

## Roles

Awakeners fill one of three combat roles:

- **DPS** — Primary damage dealers. High Attack stats, offensive card kits.
- **Tank** — Frontline defenders. High Constitution and Defense stats, protective abilities.
- **Support** — Utility and healing. Buff allies, debuff enemies, restore HP or provide shields.

## Base Stats

Every Awakener has three core stats:

| Stat | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Constitution (CON)** | Determines maximum HP |
| **Attack (ATK)** | Determines damage output |
| **Defense (DEF)** | Reduces incoming damage |

Stats increase through leveling, Enlightenment, and equipment (Wheels of Destiny and Covenants).

## Card Composition

Each Awakener comes with a personal card set:

- **4 Command Cards** — The Awakener's core abilities, drawn during battle
- **1 Spirit Awakening Card** — A powerful signature ability unique to each Awakener

During combat, Awakeners also have access to **Madness Burst**, triggered for free when Madness Points reach 100.
