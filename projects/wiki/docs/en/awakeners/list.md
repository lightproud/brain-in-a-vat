## SR Awakeners

The four starter SR Awakeners are given for free during the Prologue tutorial. Each has a corresponding SSR version that is a separate character.

| Awakener | Realm | Role | Obtain |
|----------|-------|------|--------|
| Doll (SR) | <span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> | Support | Prologue tutorial |
| Ramona (SR) | <span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> | Support | Prologue tutorial |
| Lotan (SR) | <span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> | DPS | Prologue tutorial |
| Ogier (SR) | <span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> | Defense | Prologue tutorial |
