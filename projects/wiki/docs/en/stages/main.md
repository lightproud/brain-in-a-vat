# Main Story Stages

Main story stages advance the narrative of Morimens and unlock new game features as you progress. The game currently features **9+ chapters** of main story content.

## Chapter Overview

| Chapter | Unlock | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Chapter 1 | Start of game | Tutorial and introduction |
| Chapter 2 | Clear Ch. 1 | — |
| Chapter 3 | Clear Ch. 2 | Stage 3-2 unlocks [Phase Chess](../modes/phase-chess.md) |
| Chapter 4 | Clear Ch. 3 | — |
| Chapter 5 | Clear Ch. 4 | **Remade** -- this chapter was rebuilt from scratch |
| Chapter 6 | Clear Ch. 5 | — |
| Chapter 7 | Clear Ch. 6 | — |
| Chapter 8 | Clear Ch. 7 | — |
| Chapter 9+ | Clear Ch. 8 | Ongoing story updates |

::: warning
**Chapter 5 was remade.** If you played through Chapter 5 before the rework, the story and stage layout have changed significantly. It is worth replaying for the updated narrative and rewards.
:::

## Stage Rewards

Main story stages reward:

- Player EXP
- Awakener EXP materials
- Currency
- Story unlocks
- Feature unlocks (modes, systems)

## Difficulty Tips

- **Level your team** -- Each chapter has an increasing recommended power level.
- **Match realms to enemies** -- Some boss fights favor specific realm strategies.
- **Upgrade cards** -- Card upgrades make a bigger difference than raw stat increases in many stages.
- **Use the right team composition** -- See [Team Building](../awakeners/team-building.md).
