# Resource Stages

Resource stages are the primary source of upgrade materials in Morimens. They are available daily and some rotate on a schedule.

## Resource Stage Types

| Stage Type | Drops | Availability |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| EXP Stage | Awakener EXP materials | Open daily |
| Currency Stage | In-game currency | Open daily |
| Card Upgrade Stage | Card upgrade materials | Rotating |
| Equipment Stage | Covenant and Engraving drops | Rotating |
| Ascension Stage | Awakener ascension materials | Rotating |

:::

## Farming Priority

1. **Card upgrade materials** -- Card levels have the biggest impact on combat performance.
2. **Awakener EXP** -- Level your core team to at least 25 for skill unlocks.
3. **Ascension materials** -- Required to break level caps.
4. **Equipment materials** -- Farm as needed for Covenant and Engraving upgrades.
