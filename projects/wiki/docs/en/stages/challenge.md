# Challenge Stages

Challenge stages are high-difficulty encounters in Morimens that test your team-building and combat skills. They offer premium rewards not available in regular stages.

## Challenge Modes

### Erosion Disaster Zone (Dissolution Zone)

The primary endgame multi-team challenge mode. Requires non-repeating Awakeners, Wheels, and Key Orders across teams.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Type | High-difficulty endgame challenge |
| Reset | Every 2 weeks, Thursday 15:00 |
| Unlock | Clear Main Story 4-3 (originally 5-4, lowered in v1.2) |
| Key Reward | Black Tribute, Primordial Spirit Core, Spirit Core Shards |
| Restriction | Cannot reuse the same Awakener, Wheel of Destiny, or Key Order across teams |

**Alert Levels:**

| Alert Level | Training Values | Enemy Level | Notes |
|-------------|----------------|-------------|-------|
| I | 50, 55, 60, 65, 70 | 50-58 | Entry level |
| II | 55, 60, 65, 70, 75 | 55-63 | Moderate difficulty |
| III | 60, 65, 70, 75, 80 | 60-68 | Affected by Erosion Black Tide debuffs |

**Erosion Black Tide Debuffs (Level III):**

| Debuff | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Aliemus Decay | Reduces Aliemus acquisition |
| Silver Key Oscillation | Disrupts Silver Key energy |
| Raging Seas | Deep Sea realm special effect |
| Scarlet Nurturing | Caro realm special effect |
| Realm's End | Ultra realm special effect |
| From Oblivion | Chaos realm special effect |

**Reward Thresholds:** 50, 100, 150, 225, 300, 305, 310, 315, 320, 325, 330, 335, 340, 345, 350

At 300+ training value: Black Tribute x2,500 + Rose Scrip x100,000 per tier.

### Phantasmal Dive (Dream Dive)

A roguelike ranked mode with a tier system. Significantly overhauled in v1.5.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Type | Roguelike ranked mode |
| Reset | Weekly |
| Drops | D-Jewels (Erosion Pearls), Silver |
| D-Jewel Formula | (Phantasmal level + 2) x 100 |
| Ranking | Tier-based (up to Phantasmal Legend), with weekly settlement |

**Roguelike Mechanics:**

- **Map**: Fog-of-war hex grid map, changes daily. Node types: Battle / Elite / Boss / Shop / Rest / Random Event / Treasure.
- **Inscriptions**: Obtained after defeating normal enemies (3-choose-1). Priority: Masterstroke > Inspiration > AP > Mirror Partner > Recall.
- **Creations (Artifacts)**: Obtained after defeating elite enemies (3-choose-1, usually 2 negative + 1 positive). Tiers: Silver (weak, purchasable), Gold (strong, purchasable), Cursed (strong + drawback, elite/event only). Priority: AP creations > Aliemus > Crit Rate > Card Draw > Tentacle Cap.
- **Black Ink**: Drops from battles, spent at Erosion Points. Silver creation ~50 ink, Gold ~90 ink. Spending priority: Creation > Awakening > Normal Inscription.

**Shop Items:**

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Memory Quill | 800 D-Jewels = 5/week |
| Shattered Remnant Dream | 100 D-Jewels/week |
| Stellar Remnant Dream | 25 D-Jewels/week |

### Phase Chess (PVP)

Card-based PVP combat mode, still in Beta.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Type | PVP ranked mode |
| Reset | Weekly |
| Unlock | Clear Main Story 3-2 |
| Drops | Drill Badges, Rose Scrip |
| Key Rule | Character training does not affect PVP -- all cards are at equal strength |

**PVP vs PVE Differences:**

| Aspect | PVE | PVP |
|--------|-----|-----|
| Hand Retention | Discard at turn end | Cards carry over |
| Draw Count | 4 cards | 5 cards |
| Starting AP | Fixed 5 | Starts at 3, +1/turn to cap 10 |
| Progression | Character levels matter | All cards equal strength |
| Dead Ally Cards | Removed | Become "Teardrop" (0 cost, restore 10 energy) |

**Death Duel**: When both players reach 10 AP cap, automatically applied. Layer 1 reduces healing/shields; Layer 2 sets all healing/shields to 1 -- forcing a winner.

### Other Challenge Modes

| Mode | Type | Drops | Description |
|------|------|-------|-------------|
| Lightless Realm | Star Upgrade Challenge | Lightless Currency | Unlocks Awakener star upgrades (2/3/4/5 stars). Also unlockable via Main Story 2-5. |
| D-Effect Zone | Challenge Dungeon | Lightless Currency | Additional Lightless Currency source. |
| Arcane Dominion | Challenge Dungeon | Investigation Badge | Investigation Badge acquisition. |
| Under the Outer Sky | New Dungeon | Various | v2.0+ new dungeon mode. |
| Door of All Things | Limited-Time Event | Wheel "Gate of Truth", exclusive avatar | Limited event. Owning Tawil / Wheel Unseen / Gate of Truth improves efficiency. |

::: tip
Challenge stages are the best source of rare materials like Primordial Spirit Cores and Black Tribute. Clear the Dissolution Zone bi-weekly and Phantasmal Dive weekly for optimal progression.
:::

::: warning
The Dissolution Zone has limited attempts per reset cycle. Do not waste attempts on underprepared teams. Practice in regular stages first.
:::
