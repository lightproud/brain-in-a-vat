# Affinity System

## Overview

The affinity system allows players to build deeper bonds with characters. Raising affinity unlocks exclusive story episodes, voice lines, and stat bonuses.

## How It Works

The affinity system in Morimens is called **Silver Core Communication** (银芯通信). Through this interface, the Keeper builds relationships with Awakeners by interacting, giving gifts, including them in battle, and completing character-specific quests.

For full details, see the [Silver Core Communication](../story/silver-core.md) page.

## Affinity Levels

| Level | Unlocked Content |
|-------|-----------------|
| 1 | Basic interaction dialogue |
| 2 | First personal story episode |
| 3 | New voice lines |
| 4 | Stat bonus (minor) |
| 5 | Second personal story episode |
| 6 | Additional voice lines |
| 7 | Stat bonus (moderate) |
| 8 | Third personal story episode |
| 9 | Special cosmetic content (expressions, profile items) |
| 10 | Final personal story episode and full stat bonus |

::: info
Exact affinity point thresholds vary by level. Higher tiers require significantly more points.
:::

## Ways to Raise Affinity

- **Gift Giving** -- Give Awakeners their preferred gifts for high affinity gains. Neutral gifts grant a small amount, while disliked gifts may grant none.
- **Interaction** -- Use the Silver Core Communication interface to interact with Awakeners daily. Small daily interactions accumulate over time.
- **Party Inclusion** -- Including an Awakener in your battle party slowly builds affinity through shared combat experience.
- **Character Quests** -- Special quests tied to each Awakener's backstory. Completing them grants substantial affinity.

## Affinity Rewards

Raising affinity grants:

- **Story Episodes** -- Personal backstory chapters that reveal each Awakener's history, motivations, and secrets
- **Voice Lines** -- New dialogue and voice clips unlocked at various affinity milestones
- **Stat Bonuses** -- Minor to moderate stat increases for the Awakener, aiding their combat performance
- **Cosmetics** -- Special expressions, profile decorations, or other visual content
