# Affinity System

## Overview

The affinity system allows players to build deeper bonds with characters. Raising affinity unlocks exclusive story episodes, voice lines, and stat bonuses.

## Affinity Levels

| Level | Affinity Required | Unlocked Content |
|-------|-------------------|-----------------|
| *To be added* | - | - |

## Ways to Raise Affinity

- **Gift Giving** - Give characters their preferred gifts
- **Interaction** - Interact with characters at the base
- **Party Inclusion** - Include the character in your battle party
- **Character Quests** - Complete character-specific quests

## Affinity Rewards

Raising affinity grants:

- Exclusive character story episodes
- Character voice lines
- Stat bonuses
- Special costumes / expressions
