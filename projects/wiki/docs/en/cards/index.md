# Card System

The card system is the core of Morimens' combat. Awakeners use cards to attack, defend, buff, and unleash devastating abilities. The game features **185+ cards** across all realms and types.

## Card Types

### Command Cards
<span class="card-type-badge card-command">Command</span>

Each Awakener has **4 Command Cards** that form the backbone of their kit. These are drawn into your hand during battle and played by spending Arithmetica.

### Spirit Awakening Cards
<span class="card-type-badge card-spirit">Spirit Awakening</span>

Each Awakener has **1 Spirit Awakening Card** — a powerful signature ability. Spirit Awakening Cards are typically high-impact plays that define the Awakener's playstyle.

### Madness Burst
<span class="card-type-badge card-madness">Madness Burst</span>

When an Awakener's **Madness Points** reach **100**, a Madness Burst is triggered **for free** (costs no Arithmetica). This is an automatic, powerful ability that can turn the tide of battle.

## Arithmetica (算力)

<span class="arithmetica-cost">Arithmetica</span> is the energy system used to play cards.

- You receive **5 Arithmetica per turn**.
- Each card has an Arithmetica cost displayed on it.
- Managing your Arithmetica budget across your hand is key to optimal play.

::: tip
Not all cards need to be played every turn. Holding cards for a better turn or saving Arithmetica for a high-cost Spirit Awakening Card is often the correct play.
:::

## Madness Points (狂气)

- Madness Points build up during combat through taking damage and playing certain cards.
- At **100 Madness Points**, the Awakener's Madness Burst triggers automatically and for free.
- After triggering, Madness Points reset to 0 and begin building again.

## Silver Key

- Silver Key energy accumulates during battle.
- At **1000 Silver Key**, you can activate a **Key Order** — a powerful ability tied to your equipped Key Order card.
- See [Key Orders](../key-orders/index.md) for details.

## Card Count

Morimens features **185+ cards** across all Awakeners and realms, with more added as new characters are released.
