# Proliferation Frenzy

**Proliferation Frenzy** (融灾禁区 / Erosion Disaster Zone) is a multi-team endgame challenge mode in Morimens.

## Overview

Proliferation Frenzy is the ultimate test of your roster depth. It requires assembling multiple teams using non-duplicate Awakeners, Wheels, and Key Orders to clear five sequential challenge stages.

## Requirements

- **Multiple Teams**: Build several distinct teams — no Awakener, Wheel, or Key Order can be used more than once across all teams
- **Roster Depth**: Success depends on having a deep roster of built characters
- **Strategic Planning**: Team composition across all slots matters more than any single team

## Tips

- Spread your strongest Awakeners across teams rather than stacking one team
- Plan your Wheel and Key Order assignments before starting
- Consider which stages benefit most from specific Realm synergies
- Having at least 2-3 fully invested teams is recommended before attempting
