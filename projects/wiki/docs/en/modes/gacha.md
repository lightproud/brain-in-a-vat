# Gacha / Summoning

The gacha system is how players obtain new Awakeners, Wheels of Destiny, and other items in Morimens. The game features a relatively generous SSR rate and a low pity threshold.

## Pull Rates

| Rarity | Rate |
|--------|------|
| <span class="rarity-ssr">SSR</span> | **3%** |
| <span class="rarity-sr">SR</span> | ~15% |
| <span class="rarity-r">R</span> | ~82% |

::: tip
The SSR rate was **increased from 1% to 3%** during development, making Morimens one of the more generous gacha games on the market.
:::

## Pity System

| Pity Type | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| Hard Pity | **30 pulls** |

- If you do not pull an SSR within **30 pulls**, the 30th pull is guaranteed to be SSR.
- Pity counter carries over between banners of the same type.

## Banner Types

| Banner | Contents | Pity |
|--------|----------|------|
| Limited Banner | Featured rate-up Awakener + Wheel of Destiny | 30-pull pity |
| Standard Banner | Permanent pool of Awakeners and equipment | 30-pull pity |

## Summoning Advice

1. **Save for limited banners** -- Limited Awakeners are only available during their banner period.
2. **Plan around pity** -- With a 30-pull pity, budget your pulls to guarantee at least one SSR per banner you target.
3. **Duplicates have value** -- Duplicate Awakeners feed into the [Enlightenment](../awakeners/enlightenment.md) system, granting up to 5 levels of power boosts.
4. **Wheels matter** -- An Awakener's signature [Wheel of Destiny](../wheels/index.md) significantly boosts their power. Consider pulling for both.

::: warning
The standard banner is less efficient for targeting specific characters. Spend your premium currency on limited banners for the Awakeners you want most.
:::
