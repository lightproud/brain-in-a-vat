# Phase Chess

**Phase Chess** is Morimens' fair PvP mode. All player stats are equalized, ensuring victories are determined purely by card play skill and strategy.

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Mode Type | Competitive PvP |
| Unlock | Clear Stage **3-2** |
| Stat Differences | **None** -- all stats are equalized |
| Win Condition | Reduce opponent's HP to 0 |

## Fair Play Design

::: tip
Phase Chess is designed to be completely fair. No Awakener levels, Enlightenment, Wheels of Destiny, Covenants, or Engravings affect Phase Chess stats. Winning is entirely about skill.
:::

## Rules

### Energy System

| Aspect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Starting Energy | **3** |
| Energy Per Turn | **+1** |
| Maximum Energy | **10** |

Energy in Phase Chess replaces Arithmetica and follows its own rules:

- You begin with 3 energy on the first turn.
- Each subsequent turn, you gain +1 maximum energy (Turn 2 = 4, Turn 3 = 5, etc.).
- Energy caps at **10** per turn.

### Card Draw

| Aspect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Cards Drawn Per Turn | **5** |
| Maximum Hand Size | **10** |

- You draw 5 cards at the start of each turn.
- Your hand can hold a maximum of 10 cards. Excess cards drawn beyond 10 are discarded.

### No Stat Differences

All participants in Phase Chess have:

- Equalized ATK, DEF, and CON stats.
- No equipment bonuses (Wheels, Covenants, Engravings are ignored).
- No Enlightenment bonuses.
- Card effects function at their base values.

## Strategy Tips

1. **Energy curve** -- Early turns have limited energy. Play efficient low-cost cards early; save powerful plays for later turns when energy is higher.
2. **Hand management** -- With a draw of 5 and hand limit of 10, plan which cards to hold and which to play each turn.
3. **Card knowledge** -- Since stats are equalized, knowing all card effects and their interactions is the primary competitive advantage.
4. **Realm matchups** -- Different realm strategies have different strengths in PvP. Learn the matchup dynamics.

::: warning
Phase Chess unlocks after clearing Stage 3-2. Progress through the main story to access this mode.
:::
