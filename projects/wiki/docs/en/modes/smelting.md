# Smelting Room

The **Smelting Room** is Morimens' crafting system. Players combine materials to create useful items, equipment, and upgrade resources.

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Mode Type | Crafting |
| Unlock | Main Story progression |
| Input | Raw materials gathered from stages and events |
| Output | Creations (crafted items), Covenants, Engravings, materials |

## How It Works

1. **Gather materials** -- Collect crafting materials from stages, Investigation, and events.
2. **Select a recipe** -- Choose what you want to craft from available recipes.
3. **Craft** -- Spend materials and wait for the crafting timer (if applicable).
4. **Collect** -- Retrieve your crafted item.

## Craftable Items

| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| Creations | Unique crafted items with various effects |
| Covenants | Universal equipment pieces |
| Engravings | Enhancement items |
| Materials | Converted or upgraded materials |

See [Creations](../items/creations.md) for a full list of craftable items.

## Crafting Tips

- **Prioritize upgrades** -- Craft materials you need for your main team's progression.
- **Check recipes regularly** -- New recipes may unlock as you progress through the story.
- **Do not waste rare materials** -- Plan your crafts before spending limited resources.

::: tip
The Smelting Room is a key source of Covenants and Engravings for free-to-play players. Check it regularly for useful recipes.
:::
