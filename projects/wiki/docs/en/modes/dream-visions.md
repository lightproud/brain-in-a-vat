# Dream Visions

**Dream Visions** (幻梦深潜 / Dream Dive) is a roguelite challenge mode in Morimens with a ranked tier system.

## Overview

Dream Visions is the primary competitive PvE mode, featuring roguelite mechanics with a ranked progression system. Players face increasingly difficult challenges while building their deck from scratch.

## Key Features

- **Ranked Tiers**: Progression-based ranking system with rewards per tier
- **Roguelite Format**: Build your deck as you advance through stages
- **v1.5 Overhaul**: The mode received a major rework in v1.5, including rebalanced creatures and a refreshed structure
- **Full Creature Set**: All available creatures are included with balance adjustments

## Strategy Tips

- The Royal Four (Ramona, Lotan, Doll, Ogier) and Deep Dive Trio (Aurita, Erica, Aigis) are common baseline team compositions
- Focus on synergy within a single Realm for optimal performance
- Adapt your deck strategy based on the specific modifiers each run presents
