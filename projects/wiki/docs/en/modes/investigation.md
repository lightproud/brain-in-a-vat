# Investigation

**Investigation** is Morimens' roguelite exploration mode. Players navigate a hex-based map, encountering enemies, events, and rewards as they progress through procedurally arranged stages.

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Mode Type | Roguelite PvE |
| Map | Hex-tile grid |
| Progression | Procedural; each run is different |
| Rewards | Cards, materials, Engravings, currency |

## How Investigation Works

1. **Enter a run** -- Select your team of up to 4 Awakeners.
2. **Navigate the hex map** -- Move tile by tile, choosing your path.
3. **Encounter tile events** -- Each tile has an event type (combat, treasure, shop, etc.).
4. **Build your deck** -- Acquire new cards during the run to strengthen your deck.
5. **Reach the boss** -- Defeat the final encounter to complete the Investigation.

## Tile Types

| Tile | Icon | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| Combat | Sword | Battle enemies; rewards cards and materials |
| Elite Combat | Red Sword | Harder fight; better rewards |
| Treasure | Chest | Free item or card reward |
| Shop | Coin | Spend currency earned during the run to buy cards or items |
| Event | Question Mark | Random event with choices; can be beneficial or risky |
| Rest | Campfire | Heal your team or upgrade a card |
| Boss | Skull | Final encounter of the Investigation |

## Stage Assists

During Investigation, **Stage Assists** provide temporary buffs or effects that last for the duration of the run:

- Stage Assists are offered at certain checkpoints.
- Choose from a selection of assists to complement your team and deck strategy.
- Assists do not carry over between runs.

::: tip
The rest tile choice between healing and upgrading a card is important. If your HP is healthy, always choose the card upgrade -- permanent power increases are more valuable than temporary healing.
:::

## Deckbuilding During Investigation

Investigation is where your deckbuilding skills matter most:

- You start with your Awakeners' base cards.
- Acquire additional cards from combat rewards, treasure tiles, and shops.
- Synergy matters -- pick cards that complement your realm strategy rather than taking every card offered.

::: warning
Adding too many cards to your deck dilutes your draw pool. Be selective; sometimes skipping a card reward is the correct choice.
:::

## Rewards

| Reward Type | Source |
|-------------|--------|
| Cards | Combat tiles, treasure tiles |
| Materials | Combat tiles, completion bonus |
| Engravings | Elite combat, boss |
| Currency | Various tiles |
