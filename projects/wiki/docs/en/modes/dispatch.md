# Dispatch

The **Dispatch** system allows players to send Awakeners on timed missions to gather resources passively.

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Mode Type | Idle / Passive |
| Unlock | Main Story progression |
| Duration | Varies by mission |
| Rewards | Materials, currency, EXP |

## How Dispatch Works

1. **Select a dispatch mission** from the available list.
2. **Assign Awakeners** to the mission. Some missions have realm or role requirements for bonus rewards.
3. **Wait** for the mission timer to complete.
4. **Collect rewards** when the timer expires.

::: tip
Dispatched Awakeners are still available for combat and other modes. Dispatch does not lock characters out of your roster.
:::

## Mission Types

| Mission Type | Duration | Primary Reward |
|-------------|----------|----------------|
| Short Mission | 2-4 hours | Basic materials |
| Medium Mission | 6-8 hours | Intermediate materials, currency |
| Long Mission | 12-20 hours | Rare materials, bonus rewards |

## Bonus Rewards

Some dispatch missions offer bonus rewards when specific conditions are met:

- **Realm match** -- Sending Awakeners whose realm matches the mission's theme.
- **Role match** -- Sending the recommended role type (DPS, Tank, Support).
- **High level** -- Higher-level Awakeners may increase reward quality.

## Tips

- **Set long missions before sleep** -- Maximize passive gains during downtime.
- **Match requirements** -- Always check if the mission has bonus conditions and try to fulfill them.
- **Rotate Awakeners** -- Spread dispatch usage across your roster for efficiency.
