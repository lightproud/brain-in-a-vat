# Consciousness Dive

**Consciousness Dive** (意识潜游) is an exploration game mode in Morimens that includes special stages such as "Fan-side Night Talk" (扇下夜谈).

## Overview

Consciousness Dive offers unique challenge stages with special mechanics and narrative elements. These stages differ from standard exploration by introducing puzzle-like encounters and story-driven objectives.

## Features

- Special stages with unique mechanics
- Narrative-driven content
- Distinct from standard roguelite exploration
