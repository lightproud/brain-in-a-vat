# Key Orders

**Key Orders** (钥令) are powerful abilities activated by spending **1000 Silver Key** energy during combat. They serve as a secondary ultimate ability system alongside Madness Burst.

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Activation Cost | 1000 Silver Key |
| Trigger | Manual activation when Silver Key is full |
| Source | Gacha, events, progression rewards |
| Effect | Varies by Key Order equipped |

## How Key Orders Work

1. **Silver Key accumulates** during battle through playing cards, taking damage, and realm-specific effects.
2. When Silver Key reaches **1000**, the Key Order becomes available.
3. **Activate manually** to trigger the equipped Key Order's effect.
4. Silver Key resets to 0 after activation and begins building again.

::: tip
<span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> realm teams generate Silver Key faster than other realms, making Key Orders a central part of Chaos strategy. See [Chaos Realm](../realms/chaos.md).
:::

## Key Order Effects

Key Orders provide a wide range of effects:

| Effect Category | Examples |
|----------------|---------|
| Damage | Deal massive damage to all enemies |
| Healing | Restore HP to the entire team |
| Buffs | Grant team-wide ATK, DEF, or special buffs |
| Utility | Draw cards, gain Arithmetica, generate Madness Points |

## Choosing a Key Order

- **DPS teams** -- Equip a damage-focused Key Order for burst windows.
- **Survival content** -- Equip a healing or defensive Key Order.
- **Chaos teams** -- Key Orders are your primary win condition; invest in the strongest one available.

::: warning
Key Orders take time to charge. In short fights, you may never reach 1000 Silver Key. For quick battles, other strategies may be more effective.
:::
