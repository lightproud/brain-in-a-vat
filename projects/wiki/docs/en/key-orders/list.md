# Key Orders List

A complete list of all 20 Key Orders in Morimens. Key Orders are the Keeper's exclusive combat abilities, activated by spending 1000 Silver Key Energy. Introduced in v2.0.

## All Key Orders

| Key Order | Realm Affinity | Category | Effect | Source |
|-----------|---------------|----------|--------|--------|
| Mouse Wisdom | Universal | Resource | Gain 3 Arithmetica. | Clear Main Story 2-15 |
| Little Wish | Universal | Fury | Select 1 Awakener, grant them 35 Aliemus. | Main Story |
| The Tavern Door | Universal | Draw | Place 1 "Lia's Coin" (gain 2 AP and draw 2 cards) into discard pile. | Clear Multidimensional "Reversal..." |
| Injection Guard | Universal | Defense | Defense/shield related effect (unconfirmed). | Main Story |
| Eternal Obsession | Universal | Power Burst | Gain 25% temporary crit rate and 7 temporary Power. In Boss fights, if no cards played this turn, trigger 1 Death Resist; on success gain 138 shield (1x only). | Main Story |
| Unequal Exchange | Universal | Utility | Positive effect (unconfirmed). v1.1: removed "draw 1 fewer card next turn" side effect. | Main Story |
| A Momentary Dream | Universal | Draw/Utility | Draw 1 card; if it is a command card or Gnosis Awakening, set its AP cost to 0; otherwise discard and repeat. Permanently increase Realm Mastery by 25. | Main Story |
| The Final Oath | Universal | Power Burst | Gain 23 temporary Power and 25% temporary crit rate. In Boss fights, if no cards played this turn, trigger 1 Death Resist; on success gain 1 Barrier (1x only). Values at Keeper Lv60. | Expansion/Story |
| Roar of Blood and Sand | <span class="realm-badge realm-caro">Caro</span> | Fury/Power | Lose 10% current HP, select 1 Awakener to gain 25 Aliemus and gain 12 Power. Caro bonus: Embryo Fusion +25. | Expansion/Story |
| Corrosive Embrace | <span class="realm-badge realm-aequor">Aequor</span> | Resource/Defense | Gain 1 AP; next turn gain 75 shield. Aequor bonus: instead of shield, generate 1 Tentacle. | Expansion/Story |
| Brief Eternity | <span class="realm-badge realm-ultra">Ultra</span> | Card Generation | Select 1 Awakener, place 2 of their Strike and Defense cards into hand with Exhaust and Void. Ultra bonus: additionally gain 15 temporary Power and 4 temporary Guard. | Expansion/Story |
| Ashen Mist Visage | <span class="realm-badge realm-ultra">Ultra</span> | Crit/Draw | Permanently increase crit rate by 5%; place 1 "Inspiration" into hand. Ultra bonus (during Ultra turn): place 1 additional Inspiration into hand. | Expansion/Story |
| Lakeside Glance | <span class="realm-badge realm-caro">Caro</span> | Defense/Poison | Gain 60 shield; inflict Poison equal to 25% of team's shield on all enemies. Caro bonus: the more HP lost, the higher poison, up to 50% of shield value. | Expansion/Story |
| Retrospective End | Universal | Card Replay | Place an original copy of the last non-derived command card played last turn into hand, with AP cost -1, Exhaust and Void. | Expansion/Story |
| Victory of the Rose | <span class="realm-badge realm-caro">Caro</span> | Healing/Power | Restore 45 HP. Below 25% HP, gain temporary Power equal to heal amount. Caro bonus: +50% heal, no direct heal -- stored in Scarlet Furnace instead. | Expansion/Story |
| Immortal Funeral Rites | Universal | Fury/Sacrifice | All Awakeners gain 12 Aliemus; next turn start, take sacrifice equal to 5% max HP. | Expansion/Story |
| Aurita's Treasure | <span class="realm-badge realm-aequor">Aequor</span> | Draw/Tentacle | Place the 3 lowest-AP cards from draw pile into hand. Aequor bonus: additionally increase tentacle DMG by 4-8. | Expansion/Story |
| Year-End Fireworks | Universal | Potency/Skill | Select an ally, grant +1 temporary Potency and 2 different skills; discard all hand cards at end of turn. | v1.1.11 Update |
| Manor Memories | Universal | Debuff | Select an ally; inflict 1 stack of "Intoxication" on all other allies and all enemies. (Intoxication: command card/Aliemus burst DMG/heal/shield -10%, cannot be dispelled, max 2 stacks.) | Story |
| Bleeding Sacred Heart | Universal | Draw/Crit | Draw 1 highest-AP "Skill" card, grant it Readied. Pollux special: gain 5 stacks of "Sin Mark". | Story/Event |

::: tip
Key Orders pair especially well with <span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> teams due to their accelerated Silver Key generation. Pure Chaos teams auto-generate 250 Silver Key Energy per turn and can use Key Orders twice per turn. Prioritize upgrading your Key Order if you run a Chaos-focused team.
:::

## Team Recommendations

| Team Type | Recommended Key Orders | Reason |
|-----------|----------------------|--------|
| Deep Sea Tentacle | Aurita's Treasure, Corrosive Embrace | Tentacle generation and tentacle DMG bonus |
| Caro Embryo/Fusion | Roar of Blood and Sand, Lakeside Glance, Victory of the Rose | Embryo Fusion boost, HP loss synergy, Scarlet Furnace storage |
| Ultra Card Chain | Brief Eternity, Ashen Mist Visage | Extra hand cards, Inspiration assists Ultra turns |
| Power Burst | Eternal Obsession, The Final Oath | Large Power and crit rate boosts |
| Aliemus Rush | Immortal Funeral Rites, Roar of Blood and Sand, Little Wish | Quick Aliemus fill for burst |
| Universal / Beginner | A Momentary Dream, Mouse Wisdom, The Tavern Door | AP supplement and card draw, works with any team |
