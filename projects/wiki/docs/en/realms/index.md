# Designated Realms

Morimens features four **Designated Realms** (指定领域), each representing a distinct school of eldritch power. Every Awakener and most cards belong to one realm, and building your team around a shared realm activates powerful synergies.

## Realm Overview

| Realm | Theme | Core Mechanic | Playstyle |
|-------|-------|---------------|-----------|
| <span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> (混沌) | Primordial disorder | Mix realms freely; accelerate Silver Key generation | Flexible hybrid; Key Order focused |
| <span class="realm-badge realm-aequor">Aequor</span> (深海) | Abyssal deep sea | Summon and empower Tentacle entities | Summon-based sustained damage |
| <span class="realm-badge realm-caro">Caro</span> (血肉) | Flesh and mutation | Create and consume Embryo cards | Burst combos; hand management |
| <span class="realm-badge realm-ultra">Ultra</span> (超维) | Higher dimensions | Gain extra turns from playing cards | Speed chains; low-cost card spam |

## Realm Synergy

When your team is composed of Awakeners from the same realm, realm-specific synergy bonuses activate. These bonuses enhance the core mechanic of that realm.

::: tip
<span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> Awakeners can be slotted into any realm team without breaking synergy, making them ideal flexible picks.
:::

## Choosing a Realm

- **New players** -- Start with whichever realm your best Awakeners belong to.
- **Roster building** -- Over time, aim to build at least one functional team for each realm.
- **Content adaptation** -- Some stages and bosses are easier with specific realm strategies.
