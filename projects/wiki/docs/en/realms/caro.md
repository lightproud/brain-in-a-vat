# Caro (血肉)

<span class="realm-badge realm-caro">Caro</span> is the realm of flesh and mutation. Caro Awakeners specialize in creating **Embryo** cards and consuming them for devastating burst effects.

## Core Identity

Caro is a combo-oriented realm built around a two-step cycle: generate Embryo cards, then consume them for powerful effects. Mastering this cycle requires careful hand management and turn planning.

## Key Mechanics

### Embryo Creation

Caro Command Cards and effects generate **Embryo** cards that are added to your hand.

- Embryo cards are temporary cards created during battle.
- They take up space in your hand alongside your regular cards.
- Multiple Embryo cards can be held simultaneously.

### Embryo Consumption

Other Caro cards consume Embryo cards from your hand to trigger amplified effects:

| Consumption Effect | Description |
|-------------------|-------------|
| Burst Damage | Deal massive damage scaling with Embryos consumed |
| Area Damage | Convert Embryos into AoE attacks |
| Healing | Consume Embryos to restore HP |
| Buffs | Gain stat boosts based on number of Embryos consumed |

::: tip
The key to Caro gameplay is timing. Spend 1-2 turns generating Embryos, then consume them all in a single devastating burst turn.
:::

### Hand Management

Because Embryo cards occupy hand space, Caro players must manage their hand carefully:

- Too many Embryos can crowd out your regular Command Cards.
- Too few Embryos mean your consumption effects are underwhelming.
- Aim for 2-3 Embryos before triggering your burst turn.

::: warning
Embryo cards cost Arithmetica to play. Factor in the cost of both generating and consuming Embryos when planning your Arithmetica budget for the turn.
:::

## Team Building with Caro

- **Generators** -- Awakeners with cards that reliably create Embryos each turn.
- **Consumers** -- Awakeners whose cards gain massive bonuses from consuming Embryos.
- **Support** -- Characters that help manage hand size or provide Arithmetica recovery.

## Strengths and Weaknesses

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Highest burst damage potential in the game | Requires setup turns before burst |
| Satisfying combo gameplay | Hand management adds complexity |
| Strong against single targets | Embryo generation can be inconsistent |
