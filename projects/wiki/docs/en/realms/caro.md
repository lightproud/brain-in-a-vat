# Caro (血肉)

<span class="realm-badge realm-caro">Caro</span> is the realm of flesh and mutation. Caro Awakeners specialize in creating **Embryo** cards and consuming them for devastating burst effects. Currently ranked as an **S-tier realm** alongside Aequor.

## Core Identity

Caro is a combo-oriented realm built around a two-step cycle: generate Embryo cards, then consume them for powerful effects. Mastering this cycle requires careful hand management and turn planning. It has the **highest difficulty** of all four realms to play optimally.

## Key Mechanics

### Realm Talent: Embryo System

After activating the Caro realm talent, an Embryo gauge appears above the team. The gauge fills through Caro Awakener skills (Embryo Fusion). At 100%, an "Embryo" card is added to the player's hand.

### Embryo Consumption (Devour)

While holding an Embryo card, Caro Awakeners can combine it with their Aliemus Burst to trigger a "Devour" effect, consuming the Embryo for massively enhanced results. Embryos accelerate team-wide Aliemus generation and amplify burst damage.

| Consumption Effect | Description |
|-------------------|-------------|
| Burst Damage | Deal massive damage scaling with Embryos consumed |
| Area Damage | Convert Embryos into AoE attacks |
| Healing | Consume Embryos to restore HP |
| Buffs | Gain stat boosts based on number of Embryos consumed |

### Pure Caro Resonance

In a pure Caro composition, special enhanced effects are triggered.

### Embryo Management Tips

- At Realm Mastery 34+, you naturally gain +35 Embryo Fusion per turn
- End each battle with one support's energy full and Embryo Fusion at 70-100%
- Awakening priority: Aigis > Rhea > Uvhash > Faint

::: tip
The key to Caro gameplay is timing. Spend 1-2 turns generating Embryos, then consume them all in a single devastating burst turn. Caro has the hardest learning curve -- it is difficult to manage HP and Embryo advantage during early progression.
:::

## Team Building with Caro

- **Uvhash Standard Flesh** -- Uvhash + Rhea + Aigis + Faint: The standard Caro team. Rhea + Aigis are the irreplaceable "core 2" of Caro.
- **Father (Salvador) Comp** -- Salvador + Rhea + Aigis + 24/Ramona: Community-recognized Top 3 strongest lineup. Salvador + signature wheel pushed Caro to S-tier. Rhea copies Salvador's 2-cost card for HP cycling.
- **#24 Flesh Variant** -- 24 + Helot/Rhea + Aigis + Salvador: 24's Caro resonance converts AP cost into Embryo Fusion + Power bonus.

## Strengths and Weaknesses

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Highest burst damage potential in the game | Requires setup turns before burst |
| S-tier realm with Salvador comp | Hand management adds complexity |
| Strong HP cycling and sustain | Embryo generation can be inconsistent |
| Currently tied for strongest realm | Highest difficulty to play optimally |

::: warning
Embryo cards cost Arithmetica to play. Factor in the cost of both generating and consuming Embryos when planning your Arithmetica budget for the turn.
:::
