# Creations

**Creations** are items crafted in the [Smelting Room](../modes/smelting.md). They provide various utility effects and are a key part of progression in Morimens.

## How to Craft Creations

1. Open the [Smelting Room](../modes/smelting.md).
2. Select the Creation you want to craft.
3. Ensure you have the required materials.
4. Begin crafting and wait for the timer to complete.
5. Collect the finished Creation.

## Creation Categories

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Consumables | Single-use items with immediate effects |
| Permanent Upgrades | Items that provide lasting benefits |
| Exchange Items | Items used for trading or shop exchanges |

::: tip
Check the Smelting Room regularly for new recipes. Some recipes unlock as you progress through the main story or complete specific milestones.
:::
