# Creations

**Creations** are items crafted in the [Smelting Room](../modes/smelting.md). They provide various utility effects and are a key part of progression in Morimens.

## Overview

In Morimens, "Creations" (造物) primarily refers to **Artifacts** obtained during roguelike exploration runs. These are temporary relics that provide powerful passive effects for the duration of a single run. They are distinct from permanent crafted items.

Artifacts are obtained by defeating **elite enemies** during exploration. After an elite battle, you choose 1 artifact from 3 options (typically 2 with drawbacks + 1 positive). Artifacts with drawbacks still provide beneficial effects but come with a negative side effect.

## Artifact Tiers

| Tier | Cost (Shop) | Description |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Silver** (银色) | ~50 Black Sigils | Moderate passive effects. Available for purchase in exploration shops |
| **Gold** (金色) | ~90 Black Sigils | Powerful passive effects. Available for purchase in exploration shops |
| **Cursed** (诅咒) | Not sold | Strong effects paired with negative drawbacks. Only obtained from elite enemies and specific events |

::: warning
Cursed artifacts cannot be purchased from shops. They only drop from elite enemy encounters and special exploration events. Weigh the drawback carefully before accepting one.
:::

## How to Obtain Artifacts

1. **Elite Battles** -- Defeat elite enemies during roguelike exploration to choose from 3 artifacts.
2. **Exploration Shops** -- Spend Black Sigils (黑印) earned from combat to buy Silver or Gold artifacts.
3. **Special Events** -- Some random events on the exploration map offer artifacts as rewards.

## Smelting Room

The **Smelting Room** (冶炼室) is a separate facility for permanent item exchange and crafting. It is not the same as the temporary artifact system.

| Function | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Equipment exchange | Trade in items for upgraded versions |
| Material conversion | Convert surplus materials into needed ones |
| Special crafting | Craft limited items as recipes become available |

::: tip
New Smelting Room recipes unlock as you progress through the main story or complete specific milestones. Check back regularly after clearing new chapters.
:::

## Alchemy System

The **Alchemy** (炼金) system handles material synthesis:

- **3 low-tier materials = 1 higher-tier material** (e.g., 3 Pale Notes = 1 Pale File)
- **30 Mimetic Crystals = 1 advanced skill material**

## Related Systems

- **Inscriptions** (刻印) -- Defeating normal enemies lets you inscribe bonus effects onto 3 cards (choose from random options). These are separate from artifacts.
- **Level Recall** (等级回溯) -- Resets an Awakener's levels, skills, and ascension. All consumed materials are fully refunded. Breakthrough materials return as 30 Mimetic Crystals each.
