# Materials

Materials are items used to level, ascend, and upgrade Awakeners, cards, and equipment in Morimens.

## Alchemy (Synthesis System)

The alchemy system allows material synthesis:
- 3 low-tier materials = 1 higher-tier material
- 30 Mimetic Crystal = 1 Advanced Skill Supply

## Level Recall

Resetting an Awakener's level, skills, and ascension returns **all** consumed materials in full. Breakthrough materials return as 30 Mimetic Crystal each.

Focus your material farming on whatever your main team needs most. See the [Leveling Guide](../awakeners/leveling.md) and [Card Upgrades](../cards/upgrade.md) for priority recommendations.
:::
