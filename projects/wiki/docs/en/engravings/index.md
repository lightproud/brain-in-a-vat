# Engravings

**Engravings** (刻印) are enhancement items in Morimens that provide additional stat bonuses and special effects to your Awakeners. They serve as a supplementary gear layer on top of Wheels of Destiny and Covenants.

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Type | Enhancement items |
| Acquisition | Stages, events, Investigation |
| Equip Limit | Multiple slots per Awakener |
| Purpose | Supplementary stats and effects |

## How Engravings Work

- Engravings are equipped in dedicated slots on each Awakener.
- They provide **flat stat bonuses** (ATK, DEF, CON) and may include **sub-stats** or **special effects**.
- Engravings can be swapped freely between Awakeners.

## Engraving Stats

| Stat Type | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Main Stat | Primary stat bonus (fixed by Engraving type) |
| Sub-Stats | Secondary random stats rolled on the Engraving |
| Special Effect | Some Engravings grant unique passive effects |

## Obtaining Engravings

| Source | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| Stage Drops | Farm from resource and challenge stages |
| [Investigation](../modes/investigation.md) | Random drops during Investigation runs |
| Event Rewards | Limited-time event shops |
| [Smelting Room](../modes/smelting.md) | Craft specific Engravings |

## Engraving Priority

1. **Main DPS** -- Equip your strongest offensive Engravings on your primary damage dealer.
2. **Sub-DPS** -- Use moderate Engravings that boost damage or utility.
3. **Support/Tank** -- Defensive or HP-focused Engravings for survivability.

::: tip
Do not overlook sub-stats on Engravings. A lower-rarity Engraving with perfect sub-stats can outperform a higher-rarity one with bad rolls.
:::
