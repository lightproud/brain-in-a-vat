# Story & Lore

Morimens (忘却前夜, "The Night Before Oblivion") features a deeply Lovecraftian narrative where players take on the role of a **Keeper**, tasked with guiding Awakeners through a world steeped in Cthulhu Mythos horror.

## Themes

The story of Morimens draws heavily from Lovecraftian and Cthulhu Mythos themes:

- **Cosmic horror** -- Incomprehensible entities from beyond human understanding.
- **Madness** -- The Madness Points mechanic reflects the narrative theme of exposure to eldritch truth.
- **Forbidden knowledge** -- The Silver Key and Designated Realms represent different paths to forbidden power.
- **Preservation promise** -- The Keeper's central oath to protect the Awakeners.

## The Keeper

The player character is known as the **Keeper** -- a figure who has made a promise of preservation to the Awakeners. This promise forms the emotional core of the story, as you must balance the need to send Awakeners into danger against your vow to keep them safe.
