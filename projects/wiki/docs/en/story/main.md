# Main Story

::: warning Spoiler Warning
The following content contains spoilers for the main storyline of Morimens. Read at your own discretion.
:::

The main story of Morimens spans approximately **800,000 Chinese characters** of text across two major arcs and follows the Keeper's journey through a world haunted by Lovecraftian horrors. New chapters are added with major updates.

## Arc 1: Oblivion Chapter

The Oblivion Chapter covers the Keeper's early journey, from their arrival at Mythag University to discovering the truth behind the Erosion disaster.

| Chapter | Title | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Prologue | Prologue | The Keeper awakens and discovers their role at Mythag University. |
| Chapter 1 | East District Secrets | Investigation deepens: workshop manager, suspicious doctor, suspected cult. The truth centers on a controlled girl who chose to end herself to protect her loved ones. Sub-chapters 1-1 through 1-10. |
| Chapter 2 | Metamorphosis | Main story continues. |
| Chapter 3 | Chapter 3 | Main story continues. |
| Chapter 4 | Chapter 4 | Major turning point. Characters bring a rescued girl (Lily) to a sanatorium, only to discover a larger crisis lurking within. Player choices affect later story. |
| Chapter 5 | Chapter 5 | Was temporarily closed and later reopened with compensation rewards. Remade from scratch during development. |
| Chapter 6 | Chapter 6 | Main story continues. |
| Chapter 7 | Chapter 7 | Includes 7-18 "Kindling" and other Boss stages. |

The Stellar Chapter begins with v2.0 and introduces new systems including Key Orders, mixed-realm team building, and the Chaos realm rework.

## Late-Game Story Mechanics

### Erosion Black Tide

Later chapters introduce the **Erosion Black Tide** -- a special negative effect system that includes debuffs such as "Aliemus Decay" and "Silver Key Oscillation" that alter combat dynamics.

## Key Story Themes

- **The Keeper's Promise** -- The player vows to preserve and protect the Awakeners, even as they are sent into increasingly dangerous situations.
- **The Nature of Madness** -- As the story progresses, the line between sanity and madness blurs for both the Keeper and the Awakeners.
- **Eldritch Truth** -- Each chapter reveals more about the cosmic forces at work behind the world's surface.
