# Daily Tasks

A checklist of daily and weekly activities to maximize your progression in Morimens.

## Daily Checklist

| Priority | Task | Rewards | Time |
|----------|------|---------|------|
| High | Complete daily missions | Currency, materials | 10 min |
| High | Spend stamina on resource stages | Upgrade materials | 15 min |
| High | Collect Dispatch rewards and send new missions | Materials, currency | 2 min |
| High | Check Silver Core Communication interactions | Affinity points | 3 min |
| Medium | Run Investigation if stamina permits | Cards, Engravings | 15 min |
| Medium | Attempt Challenge Stages | Premium rewards | 10 min |
| Low | Check Misag Shop refresh | Bargain items | 1 min |
| Low | Phase Chess matches | Arena currency | 10 min |

## Weekly Checklist

| Task | Rewards | Reset |
|------|---------|-------|
| Weekly missions | Premium currency | Monday |
| Challenge Stage clears | Engravings, Covenants | Monday |
| Phase Chess weekly rewards | Arena currency | Monday |

## Stamina Allocation

### Early Game (Story Progression)

- 60% Main story stages
- 30% Resource stages (EXP and card materials)
- 10% Other

### Mid Game (Team Development)

- 50% Resource stages
- 30% Card upgrade material farming
- 20% Investigation runs

### Late Game (Optimization)

- 40% Card and equipment material farming
- 30% Challenge stages
- 20% Investigation runs
- 10% Miscellaneous

::: tip
Always spend your stamina before logging off. Stamina that sits at cap is stamina wasted.
:::
