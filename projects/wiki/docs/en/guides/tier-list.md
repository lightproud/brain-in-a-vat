# Tier List

::: warning
Tier lists are subjective and change with game updates, new Awakener releases, and meta shifts. Use this as a general guide, not an absolute ranking. Tier data sourced from GameKee Wiki and community consensus; may not reflect the latest version.
:::

Many recently released characters (Tawil, Hameln, Karen, Murphy, Salvador, Wanda, Aigis, Horla, Doll: Inferno, Kathigu-Ra, Mouchette, Clementine, Corposant, Castor, Pollux, Doresain, Pickman, Murphy: Fauxborn, Xu, Leigh, Helot, Miryam, Thais, Divine King Tulu, Arachne) do not yet have confirmed tier placements in the referenced tier list.

Some <span class="rarity-sr">SR</span> Awakeners at high Enlightenment levels can compete with or outperform low-Enlightenment <span class="rarity-ssr">SSR</span> characters. Do not ignore SR options.
:::

## Tier Criteria

| Tier | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| T0 | Top-tier; excels in most content; high priority for investment |
| T1 | Strong; performs well with proper team building |
| T2 | Solid; functional but outclassed by higher tiers |
| T3 | Niche; useful in specific situations but not recommended for general use |
