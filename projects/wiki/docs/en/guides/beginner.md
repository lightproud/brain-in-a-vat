# Beginner Guide

Welcome to Morimens (忘却前夜)! This guide covers everything new players need to know to get started in this roguelite deckbuilding gacha game.

## Getting Started

### 1. Reroll or Start Playing

- The SSR rate is **3%** with a **30-pull pity**. This is generous compared to most gacha games.
- Rerolling is optional but can give you a strong start with an SSR Awakener.
- Do not overlook <span class="rarity-sr">SR</span> characters -- some are excellent and easier to Enlighten.

### 2. Progress the Main Story

- Prioritize advancing the **main story** to unlock game features and modes.
- Key unlock: **Stage 3-2** unlocks [Phase Chess](../modes/phase-chess.md) (PvP).
- Story stages reward EXP, currency, and unlock new systems.

### 3. Learn the Card Combat System

Morimens is a **turn-based deckbuilding card game**, not a real-time action game:

- You get **5 Arithmetica per turn** to play cards.
- Build **Madness Points** to 100 for a free Madness Burst.
- Accumulate **Silver Key** to 1000 to activate Key Orders.
- Each Awakener has **4 Command Cards + 1 Spirit Awakening Card**.

See [Combat System](../modes/combat.md) for full details.

### 4. Build Your Team

- Teams consist of up to **4 Awakeners**.
- Match Awakeners by **Designated Realm** for synergy bonuses.
- <span class="realm-badge realm-chaos">Chaos</span> Awakeners fit into any team.
- See [Team Building](../awakeners/team-building.md).

### 5. Manage Resources

- **Stamina** -- Spend on resource stages when not progressing story.
- **Premium Currency** -- Save for limited banners with Awakeners you want.
- **EXP Materials** -- Focus on your core 1-2 DPS characters first.
- **Card Upgrade Materials** -- Upgrading cards (up to 6 levels) has a huge impact on combat.

## Early Game Priorities

1. Clear main story chapters for feature unlocks.
2. Level your main DPS Awakener to **25** (unlocks additional skill).
3. Upgrade your main DPS's core Command Cards.
4. Build a second Awakener as sub-DPS or Support.
5. Start [Investigation](../modes/investigation.md) runs for extra rewards.
6. Set up [Dispatch](../modes/dispatch.md) missions for passive income.

## Common Beginner Mistakes

::: warning
Avoid these common mistakes:
:::

- **Spreading resources too thin** -- Focus on 1-2 core Awakeners before building others.
- **Ignoring card upgrades** -- Card levels matter more than raw stats in many fights.
- **Spending premium currency on the standard banner** -- Save for limited banners.
- **Skipping daily tasks** -- Consistent daily play is the most efficient way to progress.
- **Neglecting realm synergy** -- A realm-matched team of SR Awakeners can outperform a random team of SSRs.
- **Adding every card in Investigation** -- Be selective; deck quality matters more than quantity.
