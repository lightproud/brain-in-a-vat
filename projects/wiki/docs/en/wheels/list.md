## <span class="rarity-r">R</span> Wheels

R-quality wheels were completely reworked in v2.0 and now feature effects that change exploration rules.

| Wheel Name | Awakener | Effect |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Punishment | — | Keep 50 Black Ink before Boss = +250% Death Resist. Highest rated R wheel. |
| Emergence | Celeste | R wheel for Celeste. |
| Frenzy | Coral (Shan) | R wheel for Coral. |
| Delirium | — | Reduce Black Ink through Awakening. Recommended R wheel. |
| Gluttony | — | Pairs with: Exile engraving + HP. |
| Exile | — | Pairs with: Mastery + sell status cards. |
| Enlightenment | — | Pairs with: Mastery + Silver Key. |
| Focused Mind | — | Pairs with: Silver Key + Aliemus. |
| Heart's Bastion | — | Suited for high-DEF characters like Faros or Shan. |

::: tip
Duplicate wheels cannot be equipped in the same team. After upgrading a wheel to +12, you can equip an additional SSR wheel. In v2.0+, reaching a certain progression point allows equipping an extra R or SR wheel.
:::
