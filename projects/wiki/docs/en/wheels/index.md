# Wheels of Destiny

**Wheels of Destiny** (命轮) are character-specific equipment in Morimens. Each Wheel is designed for a particular Awakener and provides stat bonuses and unique passive effects that enhance that character's kit.

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Type | Character-specific equipment |
| Acquisition | Gacha / Summoning banners |
| Equip Limit | One Wheel per Awakener |
| Tradeable | No |

## How Wheels Work

- Each Wheel of Destiny is **locked to a specific Awakener** and cannot be equipped by anyone else.
- Wheels provide **base stat bonuses** (ATK, DEF, CON) and a **unique passive effect** tailored to the Awakener's playstyle.
- Higher-rarity Wheels provide stronger stats and more impactful passives.

## Obtaining Wheels of Destiny

Wheels are primarily obtained through the gacha system:

- **Character Banners** -- Featured Awakener banners often include their signature Wheel.
- **Standard Banner** -- Some Wheels are available in the permanent pool.
- **Event Rewards** -- Occasionally awarded during special events.

::: tip
When pulling on a character banner, obtaining both the Awakener and their Wheel of Destiny significantly increases that character's power. Plan your pulls accordingly.
:::

## Wheels vs. Covenants

| Feature | Wheels of Destiny | Covenants |
|---------|------------------|-----------|
| Equip restriction | Character-specific | Universal (any Awakener) |
| Source | Primarily gacha | Various sources |
| Power level | Higher (tailored) | Lower (general) |
