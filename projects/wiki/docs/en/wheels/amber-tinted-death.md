## Effect

Crit. DMG: 21.6% Team Unique: The wielder's Base DMG dealt +20%, and STR generated +6%. After playing the wielder's Command Card, gain Temporary STR equal to 11% of the wielder's ATK, triggering up to 5 times per turn.
Amplifies the Base DMG dealt by the wielder and their STR acquisition, while continuously generating Temporary STR by playing the wielder's cards.
